## Example: Completed Persona

### Janet

*[Image: woman in her late 60s, sensibly dressed, looking slightly uncertain]*

> **"I just want to know that I've done everything right. I don't want to cause any trouble."**

---

### Background

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Age | 67 |
| Location | Semi-rural North Yorkshire, relies on public transport |
| Life situation | Retired, lives alone since her husband died two years ago. Her daughter lives 40 minutes away and helps with major decisions. |
| Life situation | Recently diagnosed with a cardiac condition. Attending regular outpatient appointments. |

---

### Goals

**Functional:**
- Get to and from her appointment without difficulty
- Understand what the doctor has told her and what she needs to do next
- Know when her next appointment is and how to prepare

**Emotional:**
- Feel that the staff know who she is and that she is not just a number
- Feel reassured that everything is under control
- Be able to tell her daughter what happened

---

### Frustrations and Pain Points

- The letters she receives use medical language she does not understand
- She often has to wait over an hour with no information about how long it will be
- She has to explain her situation from scratch every time she sees a different doctor
- The bus home doesn't always align with when she finishes, causing anxiety

---

### Behaviours

- Writes everything down in a notebook during appointments
- Calls her daughter immediately after leaving the clinic
- Arrives 30 minutes early because she worries about being late
- Asks a neighbour to help her read letters she doesn't understand

---

### Digital and Channel Use

| Channel | Comfort level | Usage pattern |
|---------|--------------|--------------|
| Smartphone | Low | Has a basic smartphone, uses it for calls and simple texts |
| Computer | Very low | Has an old laptop but rarely uses it |
| Phone calls | High — preferred | Will call the hospital if unsure about anything |
| In person | Preferred for important matters | Would rather talk to someone face to face |
| Post / letter | Uses, but finds it stressful | Relies on letters for appointment information |

---

### Supporting Quotes

> "I never know if I should be worried or not. The letter says to call if things change, but what counts as changing?"

> "I wrote it all down but then I got home and I couldn't read my own writing properly."

> "I always get there early. I'd rather wait than be late and cause a problem."
