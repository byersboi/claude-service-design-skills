## Ground Rules (suggested)

Post these at the start of every workshop:

- One conversation at a time
- Build on each other's ideas
- No idea is too wild — quantity over quality in generative phases
- All voices matter equally in this room
- Phones away unless needed for the activity
- What's said in this room stays in this room (for sensitive topics)

---

## Contingency Plans

| Scenario | Response |
|----------|---------|
| Running 10 minutes over | Cut [Activity X] — outputs from [Activity Y] are sufficient for the objective |
| Group gets stuck | Use "worst possible idea" to break the impasse, or pivot to a different stimulus |
| Dominant participant | Switch to silent individual generation; use round-robin for sharing |
| Tech failure (remote) | Have a backup: WhatsApp group, email, phone call to co-facilitator |
| Key participant drops out | Adjust group sizes; capture their absence and re-engage after |

## Post-Workshop Checklist

- [ ] Document all outputs (photos, digital export)
- [ ] Write up key themes and decisions within 24 hours (while memory is fresh)
- [ ] Send summary to participants with action log
- [ ] Add insights to research repository
- [ ] Update project plan with actions arising
- [ ] Debrief with co-facilitator — what worked, what to change
